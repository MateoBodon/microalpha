    Pre-holdout research evidence — 2026-07-11

    Status: reviewed aggregate evidence; licensed source rows are not distributed

    Selection window: 2017–2022

    Final holdout: 2023–2025, sealed throughout this campaign

    Interpretation: validation evidence only; no alpha, promotion, or live-performance claim

    This note is a public-safe summary of a frozen research campaign. Each candidate was specified before its return computation and kept or rejected using its predeclared gate. Results include implemented costs; stress results increase borrow to 600 bps and double non-borrow costs where stated.
