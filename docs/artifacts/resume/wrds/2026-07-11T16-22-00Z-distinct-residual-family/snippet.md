# CRSP-v2 distinct residual-family result

The preregistered `residual_mom_12_2__equal` candidate was the best of three
structurally eligible candidates on the 2017-2022 validation window. Its net
HAC Sharpe was `0.3198` (t-stat `1.2174`), CAGR `2.48%`, and max drawdown
`9.39%` under the unchanged $15MM portfolio, capacity constraints, point-in-time
industry neutrality, commissions, spread, impact, and 300 bps short-borrow cost.

The family is archived as a validation negative. It improved the previous
frozen candidate's HAC Sharpe by `0.0791`, below the predeclared `0.10`
replacement margin; it also trailed identical-universe classic momentum at
`0.3260`, and harsh-stress CAGR was `-0.20%` with 600 bps borrow and 2x
nonborrow costs. The 2023-2025 final holdout was not opened. This is neither a
promotion nor an alpha claim.
